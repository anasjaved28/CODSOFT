AUDIOLINK

Stream a single audio source to multiple Bluetooth devices simultaneously — no software installation required. Built with the Web Audio API.

Live Demo View on GitHub

Features

Everything you need for a seamless multi-device audio experience.

🎚️

Real-Time Audio Controls

Adjust gain, delay, and filtering per device in real time for a perfectly synchronized listening experience.

📦

No Installation Needed

Runs entirely in your browser. No apps, no plugins — just open and start routing audio instantly.

🔇

Feedback Loop Guard

Built-in safety guard detects and prevents audio feedback loops before they can disrupt your session.

🔊

Audio Limiter

Uses AudioWorkletNode to prevent signal clipping and ensure high audio fidelity across all output devices.

How It Works

Get started in just three simple steps.

1

Open the App

Visit AUDIOLINK in your browser. No login or installation required.

2

Select Your Devices

Choose the Bluetooth output devices you want to route your audio to.

3

Start Streaming

Hit play and control each device's audio independently in real time.
